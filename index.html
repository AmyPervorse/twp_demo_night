About Me

In April of 2016, my husband and I visited Chattanooga from our hometown of Atlanta. On that weekend we visited the aquarium, strolled around the city, and enjoyed some southern comfort foods at Puckettâ€™s. We fell in love with the city and decided we wanted to make Chattanooga our new home. In October of 2016, we took the leap, packed our belongings and cats, and headed to our new favorite city.

During this transition, I realized I needed to find a professional field that I could be passionate about. My Bachelors in Business Management had allowed me to explore marketing and accounting in the consumer electronics sector. While I learned and grew as a professional, I also fell in love with tech. Projects I've Created

I created this website while folowing along with the instructor during the Tech Workforce Program. The website contains unordered lists, an imbeded map, CSS styling, loops, and even a Javascript game! I used Atom to edit the code, Github to push to the repository, and Firebase to deploy to the internet.

I created this simple website in order to be considered as an appilcant for the Tech Workforce Program. I took an HTML course in high school but was unfamiliar with CSS. After taking CodeAcademy's CSS course, I was able to incorporate what I'd learned into my website.
